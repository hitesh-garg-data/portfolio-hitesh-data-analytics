# Project: Sales Overview Dashboard
**Tools Used:** Excel, Power BI Desktop  
**Problem Statement:** The business required interactive insight into regional performance, product sales, and trend analysis to guide decision-making.  
**Dataset Description:** Cleaned Excel dataset (with Date, Region, Product, Units, Price, Sales, Sales_Level) extracted and transformed via Power Query.  
**Key Insights:**  
- Top performing region: North (≈ ₹ 1,28,161)  
- Average sales per unit: ~ ₹ 72.58  
- Notable seasonal peaks: Month March showed highest sales  
**Outcome:** Delivered a fully interactive dashboard allowing management to filter by region/product and explore sales trends, ready for presentation and decision support.
